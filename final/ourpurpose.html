Our Purpose

Since 1998, Pacific Trails Resort has been striving to deliver everything a California holiday should have.
We welcome guests to experience the wild beauty of Pacific Ocean in comfortable luxury, all year long.

Our Community

Join us in our path to preach the joy of holidays dwelling in nature and unplugged from all external stress. Whether you are an intrepid explorer or simply desiring a quality family time, it is only pure happiness and relaxations that the Pacific Trails Resort seeks to induce in all our guests. To receive updates about exclusive experiences, events, new destinations and more, please register your interest.

Responsible and Natural

An established resort hotel, Pacific Trails Resort has programs featuring natural attractions operating throughout the year. Onsite professional leisure staff ensures your responsible travel to natural areas, to conserve and enhance the welfare of local ecosystem. We seek the best measures to provide our guests the most intimate retreat with nature.
